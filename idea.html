What is the BreadButter App?

The BreadButter internal app is the central nervous system of our business. It acts as a "breadboard," providing a structured and searchable home for all our deals, decisions, and discussions. The platform centralizes information that typically lives across various tools like Google Docs, Slack, Notion, and email, making our workflows more efficient and our data more accessible.

The Comms Hub: Centralized Communication

The Comms Hub is where all conversations and notes are centralized, eliminating the need to search through emails or chat threads. It's designed to capture any piece of information and link it directly to the relevant client, talent, or gig.
